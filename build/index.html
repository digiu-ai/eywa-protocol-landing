Roadmap
2021
Q1-Q2
Create blockchain laboratory DigiU.lab, starting research.
EthGlobal Marketmake Win, prise Chainlink proof of concept.
Chainlink Spring 2021 - MVP : Crosschain AMM DEX (Ethereum Rinkeby <> Binance Smart Chain testnet)
Building the Team, development product.
2021
Q3-Q4
EYWA DEX alpha in testnets: Ethereum Rinkeby, BSC, Polygon, HECO, Avalance, Solana crosschain pools and swaps.
EYWA Relayers Network open testnet:multichain gasless, DPoS network with slashing.
Starting to build a community of EYWA DEX users and developers community using EYWA Crosschain Data Protocol.
Funding round (private).
2022
Q1-Q2
Beta in mainnet: launch EYWA DEX with user’s subsidy and gasless. Light setup for Relayers Network, quantity up to 500 relayers.
Funding rounds (Private, Community Sale), TGE and launch crosschain yield farming.
Grow community and business development: adding more pools, assets and opportunites for users.
Adding new chains: Polkadot, FreeTON and more EVM chains.
2022
Q3-Q4
NFT crosschain bridge, NFT crosschain marketplace.
Adding new chains: Cosmos, Terra and more EVM chains.
Relayers Network 2.0: up to 1000 relayers and more, full DeFi oracles functional (price feeds, random number generators, etc).
Cross-chain AMM DEX and bridge protocol
